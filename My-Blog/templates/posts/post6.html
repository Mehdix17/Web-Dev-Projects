Cybersecurity and AI: A Double-Edged Sword

How AI Helps — and Threatens — Digital Security

Posted by Leena Varsani on 17/09/2023

AI plays a critical role in modern cybersecurity. Machine learning models can detect suspicious network behavior, flag phishing emails, and automate incident response. These tools help organizations stay ahead of rapidly evolving cyber threats.

However, AI can also be used by malicious actors. Deepfakes, automated hacking tools, and AI-powered social engineering are becoming new fronts in the cybersecurity war. As defenses get smarter, so do the attacks — making cybersecurity a race against time and technology.
