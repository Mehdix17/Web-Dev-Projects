What Is Computer Science ? A Beginner’s Guide

Understanding the Foundations of the Digital World

Posted by Julien Faye on 14/03/2024

Computer Science is the study of computers and computational systems. It involves understanding algorithms, data structures, software engineering, programming languages, artificial intelligence, and much more. At its core, computer science is about problem-solving — using logic and creativity to build systems that help people and businesses work smarter.

Whether you're building a mobile app, designing a secure database, or training a machine learning model, computer science provides the tools to make it possible. It's one of the fastest-growing fields globally, offering lucrative and intellectually rewarding career paths.
