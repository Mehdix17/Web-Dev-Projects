AI in Healthcare: Revolutionizing Diagnosis and Treatment

How Artificial Intelligence Is Saving Lives

Posted by Nadia Kimani on 10/12/2022

Artificial Intelligence is transforming healthcare by enabling faster diagnosis, personalized treatments, and predictive analytics. AI-powered tools can analyze medical images, detect tumors, and forecast patient deterioration, often with greater accuracy than humans.

Startups and major tech companies are collaborating with hospitals to integrate AI into radiology, pathology, and patient care. While promising, challenges remain in terms of data privacy, algorithm bias, and ensuring accountability in life-critical applications.
