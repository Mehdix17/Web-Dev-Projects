# Product Brief: Slidely

## Executive Summary

Slidely is a presentation design agency portfolio website built to attract freelance clients and establish a reputation for accessible, high-quality work. Founded by a designer with an AI/technical background, Slidely bridges the gap between template-driven tools and expensive agencies—offering custom, strategic presentation design.

The website showcases approximately 7 diverse presentation projects spanning pitch decks, corporate materials, and educational content. Interested clients are directed to Upwork and Fiverr profiles where pricing and project details are discussed.

This is a reputation-first play: build credibility through quality work, grow the portfolio, and establish Slidely as a reliable choice for startups and businesses that need presentations that actually communicate.

## The Problem

**For clients:** Finding presentation design help is a frustrating middle-ground problem.

- **DIY tools** (Canva, Beautiful.ai) promise speed but deliver generic results. They can't handle complex business narratives, strategic messaging, or custom data visualization. The output looks like everyone else's.
- **Premium agencies** charge $6,000-$15,000—pricing out startups, students, and small businesses that still need professional work.
- **Freelancer marketplaces** are a gamble: inconsistent quality, communication breakdowns, hidden costs, and the risk of ghosting mid-project.

**For the founder:** Existing platforms (Upwork, Fiverr) bury new profiles under established competition. A standalone portfolio website establishes credibility before the first conversation.

The cost of the status quo: clients settle for mediocre presentations that fail to persuade, and talented designers struggle to break through the noise.

## The Solution

**Slidely** — a portfolio website that demonstrates craft, builds trust, and converts visitors into clients.

The experience:
1. **Discover** — Visitors browse a curated gallery of presentation work, filtered by type (pitch decks, corporate, educational)
2. **Evaluate** — Each project showcases the design process, strategic thinking, and final outcome
3. **Trust** — About page establishes credibility
4. **Connect** — Clear call-to-action directs to Upwork/Fiverr profiles for inquiry and hiring

The positioning is **accessible quality**: professional-grade work at fair rates, with the reliability and communication that marketplace freelancers often lack.

## What Makes This Different

| Competitor Type | Their Approach | Slidely's Edge |
|-----------------|--------------|----------------|
| DIY Tools | Self-serve templates | Human strategy + custom design |
| Premium Agencies | $6K-$15K, business outcomes focused | Accessible pricing, personal attention |
| Marketplace Freelancers | Volume, inconsistent quality | Curated portfolio, clear communication |
| Speed Shops | 8-24 hour turnaround | Quality-first, reasonable timelines |

**Bonus: Technical fluency** — The founder's AI/technical background means:
- Understanding data visualization and complex information design
- Speaking the language of tech clients and startups
- Ability to handle technical subject matter without extensive hand-holding

This isn't "AI-powered design"—it's a designer who understands technology, which is rare in the presentation design space.

## Who This Serves

**Primary: Early-stage startups and small businesses**
- Need pitch decks for fundraising
- Need corporate presentations for sales/partnerships
- Need educational materials for training or marketing
- Can't afford agency rates but need professional quality
- Value clear communication and reliable delivery

**Secondary: Students, academics, and non-profits**
- Need educational content designed
- Limited budgets but high standards
- Appreciate the student-to-student connection

**Tertiary: Marketing agencies and consultants**
- Need overflow presentation support
- Value a reliable freelancer relationship

## Who This Does NOT Serve

- Clients seeking $50 slide decks (quality requires investment)
- Rush jobs requiring sub-48 hour turnaround without proper briefing
- Clients unwilling to provide content/strategic direction

## Success Criteria

**Phase 1 — Launch (Target: 4-6 weeks)**
- [ ] Portfolio website live with 5-7 showcased projects
- [ ] All core pages complete: Home, Portfolio, About, Process, Contact
- [ ] Portfolio filtering functional (by project type)
- [ ] Contact flow directs to Upwork/Fiverr profiles
- [ ] Performance: <2s LCP, 90+ Lighthouse score

**Phase 2 — Validation (Target: 3 months post-launch)**
- [ ] First inbound inquiry through portfolio website
- [ ] 3+ projects completed for portfolio clients
- [ ] Client testimonial collected
- [ ] 100+ monthly portfolio views

**Phase 3 — Growth (Target: 6-12 months)**
- [ ] Portfolio expanded to 10+ projects
- [ ] Consistent monthly inquiries (2+)
- [ ] Reputation established on freelance platforms

**Key Metrics:**
- Portfolio views per week (target: 25+ by month 3)
- Contact/CTA click-through rate (target: 5%+)
- Conversion rate: visitor → inquiry → project
- Client satisfaction (testimonials, repeat business)

## Scope

**In Scope (MVP):**
- Portfolio gallery with category filtering (pitch deck, corporate, educational)
- Individual project pages with case study details
- About page with founder story and technical background
- Process page explaining how Slidely works
- Contact page with links to Upwork/Fiverr profiles
- Responsive design, optimized images, no paid dependencies
- SEO-friendly structure with Open Graph meta tags

**Out of Scope (for now):**
- Pricing page (discussed in DMs/platforms)
- Blog or content marketing
- Client portal or project management
- Direct payment processing
- Real-time chat or booking system
- Multi-language support

**Technical Stack:**
- Framework: Next.js (static export)
- Hosting: Vercel (free tier)
- Styling: Tailwind CSS
- Images: WebP format, lazy loading, responsive srcset
- SEO: Semantic HTML, meta tags, sitemap

## Vision

If Slidely succeeds, it becomes:

**Year 1:** A reliable freelance income source with a growing portfolio and satisfied client base. Reputation established on Upwork/Fiverr with portfolio as the credibility anchor.

**Year 2-3:** A recognized name in the presentation design space—perhaps expanding to offer templates, courses, or a small team. The technical background becomes a platform for thought leadership at the intersection of design and AI.

**The bigger picture:** Slidely proves that accessible pricing and professional quality aren't mutually exclusive. It becomes a case study in building a service business through craft, communication, and strategic positioning.
